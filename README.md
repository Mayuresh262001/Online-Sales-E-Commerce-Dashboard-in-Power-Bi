This project presents an end-to-end analytical dashboard for an **Online Sales E-Commerce Platform**, designed to convert raw transactional data into **actionable business insights**.
The dashboard enables decision-makers to track **sales performance**, analyze **profitability**, understand **customer behavior**, and evaluate **operational efficiency** across regions and product categories.

**üìä Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

**Total Sales Revenue**
A consolidated view of overall business revenue.

**Total Profit & Profit Margin (%)**
Measures financial health and operational efficiency.

**Total Orders & Quantity Sold**
Tracks customer demand and sales volume.

**Average Order Value (AOV)**
Analyzes customer purchasing power and basket size.

**Shipping Performance**
Evaluates delivery timelines and operational bottlenecks.
